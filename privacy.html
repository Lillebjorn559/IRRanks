IRRanks — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

IRRanks is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

IRRanks collects and stores the following data when you use the bot:

  • Discord User ID — to identify you across servers
  • iRacing Customer ID — to fetch your iRacing stats
  • iRating and Safety Rating — to display stats and generate monthly reports
  • Display Name — if set by an admin via the changename command

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide IRRanks functionality, including:

  • Updating your Discord nickname with your current iRating and license
  • Displaying leaderboards and monthly reports
  • Announcing split wins in your server

We do not sell, share, or use your data for any purpose other than providing the bot's features.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on a private server. Only the bot operator has access to the stored data. Data is kept only as long as you are linked to the bot. You can remove your data at any time by using the !IRR unlink command.

5. Third Party Services

IRRanks uses the iRacing API to fetch member statistics. By linking your iRacing account, you acknowledge that your public iRacing data will be fetched and displayed. IRRanks also operates on the Discord platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Remove your data at any time using !IRR unlink
  • Request information about what data is stored about you
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of IRRanks after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact us via the IRRanks Discord server.
